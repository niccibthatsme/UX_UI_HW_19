I'm Nicole, and in 2022, I transitioned from an 18-year career as a technical designer in the fashion industry to become a UX designer. This change reflects my unwavering passion for design, empathetic approach to understanding user needs, and ability to merge artistic creativity with technical proficiency. I take pride in creating user experiences that combine aesthetics and functionality, resulting in visually appealing and intuitive designs. My problem-solving approach involves empathy and thorough research, recognizing the importance of user needs and motivations. With my background as a technical designer, I bring a unique blend of creativity and attention to detail, enabling me to design visually stunning and practical experiences.
